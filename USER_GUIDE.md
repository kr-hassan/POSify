# POS System User Guide

## Getting Started

### Login Credentials

- **Admin**: `admin@pos.com` / `password` (Full access to everything)
- **Manager**: `manager@pos.com` / `password` (Can manage products, sales, purchases)
- **Cashier**: `cashier@pos.com` / `password` (Can only use POS and view products)

## Navigation

After logging in, you'll see a sidebar on the left with the following options:

### 1. Dashboard
- View today's sales, purchases, and expenses
- See low stock products
- View recent sales
- Check monthly statistics

### 2. POS (Point of Sale)
- **This is where you make sales!**
- Search for products by name, SKU, or barcode
- Add products to cart
- Select customer (optional)
- Apply discounts
- Choose payment method
- Complete the sale

### 3. Products
- **View all products**: Click "Products" in sidebar
- **Add new product**: Click "Add Product" button (top right)
- **Edit product**: Click the pencil icon next to any product
- **Delete product**: Click the trash icon (only if no sales/purchases exist)

### 4. Categories
- **View categories**: Click "Categories" in sidebar
- **Add category**: Click "Add Category" button
- **Edit category**: Click the pencil icon
- **Delete category**: Only if no products are in that category

### 5. Customers
- **View customers**: Click "Customers" in sidebar
- **Add customer**: Click "Add Customer" button
- **Edit customer**: Click the pencil icon
- Search customers by name, phone, or email

### 6. Suppliers
- **View suppliers**: Click "Suppliers" in sidebar
- **Add supplier**: Click "Add Supplier" button
- **Edit supplier**: Click the pencil icon

### 7. Sales
- **View all sales**: Click "Sales" in sidebar
- **Filter by date**: Use the date filters
- **View invoice**: Click on any sale to see details
- **Print invoice**: Opens in new window for printing

### 8. Purchases
- **View purchases**: Click "Purchases" in sidebar
- **Add purchase**: Click "Add Purchase" button
- This increases product stock automatically

### 9. Expenses
- **View expenses**: Click "Expenses" in sidebar
- **Add expense**: Click "Add Expense" button
- Track business expenses by category

### 10. Reports
- **Sales Report**: View sales by date range
- **Product Report**: See which products sell best
- **Stock Report**: Check inventory levels
- **Profit Report**: Calculate profit margins
- **Customer Due Report**: See customers who owe money

## How to Use the POS System

### Step-by-Step: Making a Sale

1. **Go to POS**: Click "POS" in the sidebar
2. **Search for Product**: 
   - Type product name, SKU, or scan barcode
   - Press Enter or click "Search"
   - Products will appear below
3. **Add to Cart**:
   - Click "Add" button on any product
   - Product appears in cart on the right
4. **Adjust Quantity**:
   - Change the number in the quantity field
   - Press Enter to update
5. **Select Customer** (Optional):
   - Choose from dropdown or leave as "Walk-in Customer"
6. **Apply Discount** (Optional):
   - Enter discount amount
   - Choose $ (flat) or % (percentage)
7. **Choose Payment Method**:
   - Cash, Card, or Mobile
8. **Enter Paid Amount**:
   - System calculates change automatically
9. **Complete Sale**:
   - Click "Complete Sale" button
   - Invoice will open in new window
   - Stock is automatically reduced

### Tips for POS:
- Use barcode scanner: Just scan and press Enter
- Quick search: Type product name and press Enter
- Remove items: Click trash icon in cart
- Clear cart: Click "Clear Cart" button

## Setting Up Your Store

### Initial Setup (Recommended Order):

1. **Create Categories First**
   - Go to Categories → Add Category
   - Examples: Electronics, Clothing, Food, etc.

2. **Add Products**
   - Go to Products → Add Product
   - Fill in all required fields:
     - Name, SKU (unique), Barcode (optional)
     - Category, Cost Price, Sell Price
     - Stock quantity, Alert quantity
     - Tax % (if applicable)

3. **Add Customers** (Optional but recommended)
   - Go to Customers → Add Customer
   - Add name, phone, email, address

4. **Add Suppliers** (For purchases)
   - Go to Suppliers → Add Supplier
   - Add name and phone

5. **Start Making Sales!**
   - Go to POS and start selling

## Common Tasks

### Adding Stock (Purchase)
1. Go to Purchases → Add Purchase
2. Select supplier
3. Add products with quantities and cost prices
4. Enter paid amount
5. Stock is automatically increased

### Checking Low Stock
- Dashboard shows low stock products
- Or go to Products and filter by "Low Stock"

### Viewing Sales History
- Go to Sales
- Filter by date range
- Click on any sale to see details

### Printing Invoice
- After completing a sale, invoice opens automatically
- Or go to Sales → Click on any sale → View Invoice
- Use browser print (Ctrl+P) for thermal printer

## Troubleshooting

### Can't find a product in POS?
- Make sure product is "Active" (check in Products)
- Check if product has stock
- Try searching by SKU or barcode

### Stock not updating?
- Check if purchase was completed successfully
- Verify product exists

### Can't delete product/category?
- Make sure no sales or purchases reference it
- Delete related records first

### Invoice not printing?
- Use browser print (Ctrl+P or Cmd+P)
- Select 80mm paper size
- Check printer settings
